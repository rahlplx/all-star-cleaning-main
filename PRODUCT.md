# Product Context — All Star Cleaning Ottawa

## Register
**brand** — This is a marketing surface: a local service business landing page. Design IS the product. A visitor's impression directly drives lead conversion.

## Users
- Homeowners and property managers in the Ottawa region seeking exterior cleaning services
- Bilingual audience (English and French) with strong local identity
- Mobile-first users: most leads come from phones while on-the-go or at the property
- Decision-makers comparing 2-3 local services before calling for a quote

## Product Purpose
Convert visitors into phone calls and quote requests for residential and commercial exterior cleaning services (window, gutter, pressure washing, siding, snow removal) across 44 Ottawa-area locations.

## Brand Voice
Three words: **reliable, local, trustworthy**. Not corporate or slick. Warm confidence from someone who shows up on time, knows Ottawa winters, and guarantees the work. Approachable expertise.

## Tone
- Direct and confident, not salesy
- Local pride without provincialism
- Professional without being stiff
- Reassuring: "We've got this"

## Strategic Principles
1. **Trust first**: Every page must communicate reliability within 3 seconds
2. **Local credibility**: Ottawa-specific knowledge is our differentiator
3. **Frictionless contact**: The path from landing to calling must be 1 tap on mobile
4. **Bilingual respect**: French and English are first-class, not translations

## Anti-References
- Corporate cleaning franchises with generic stock photos
- SaaS-style landing pages with hero metrics and gradient text
- Overly playful or cartoonish service sites
- Generic blue-and-white contractor websites

## Key Surfaces
- Homepage: first impression, must establish trust and drive quote requests
- Service detail pages: feature-rich, SEO-optimized, with sticky CTA
- Location+service combo pages: 440 programmatic SEO pages, must feel unique despite template reuse
- Contact page: conversion endpoint, form + phone prominent
- Reviews page: social proof (currently placeholder, needs warmth)
